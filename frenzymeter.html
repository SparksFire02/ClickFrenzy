Frenzy Meter
The frenzy meter starts empty. As you click, it gets filled up, and maxes out at 50 blocks.
The frenzy meter activates one of the following skills:
x2 Multiplier
Doubles your score for 10 seconds
Rarity: 0.800
Touch of Midas
Turns everything to gold, with Midas's love of x2 multiplier for 5 seconds
Rarity: 0.195
Purge
Purge everything!!! with x5 multiplier
Rarity: 0.005
Note: while a skill is running, you cannot activate the meter even if it is full
